Making New Friends

Zaya and Miguel are friends.

One day, they went to a park after school.

They talk about their new friend, Riley.

Riley and Miguel like soccer.

Zaya didn't like soccer.

She felt sad.

At home, Zaya told her dad how she felt.

Zaya was scared she would feel left out.

Zaya's dad told her that it was good to make new friends.

Zaya went outside to see Miguel and Riley.

Riley gave Zaya a high five.

The three new friends played all afternoon!

Then they said goodbye as they went inside.
